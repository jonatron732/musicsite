What is DCYO

DCYO, also known as Douglas County Youth Orchestra is an orchestra for youth who are at the beginner to intermediate level of ability and want to be a part of a comunity of like minded individual. DCYO is a great place to make friends and improve your playing skills!

My experience

I am the current conductor of the Douglas County Youth Orchestra and was one of the adopters of this orchestra. Me and a couple other parents came together to take of the orchestra when the previous director and conductor left. Ever since then we have been able to grow the orchestra into what it is today even through covid and the challenges it brought.
