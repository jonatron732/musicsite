How Does my Studio Work?

I am a year-round teacher and I schedule in six-month periods. There are 23 lessons and 1 studio recital from January through June.

Pricing

30 minute lesson: $120 per month or $720 Payment for six months

45 minute lesson: $180 per month or $1080 payment for six months

60 minute lesson: $240 per month or $1320 payment for six months

These fees are what is expected for six months. You can pay by month or for all six months at once. This is a six-month tuition, and not a lesson-by-lesson fee. Some months may have 5 lessons and some months may have 3 lessons. So some months are more expensive, and some months less expensive.

The lesson fees are due by the first lesson of the month. I do not charge late fees like other teachers might do, but please be considerate and pay on time. If for any reason there are financial issues or you prefer paying on a different date of the month, PLEASE let me know!

If lessons are canceled for any reason by student or teacher, a makeup can be requested. Makeup lessons must be rescheduled within a 1-week time period of their missed lesson during my makeup lesson availability.
